About the Founder

Kopano Maketekete

Electrical & Computer Engineering, University of Cape Town

I am a passionate researcher and hardware builder deeply invested in the architecture of reconfigurable systems. My work focuses on bridging the gap between high-end electronics and the rugged realities of remote environments.

Agrionics Co. was born from the realization that standard off-the-shelf IoT hardware simply fails when subjected to the power limitations, vast distances, and environmental stresses of the African edge.

By leveraging bare-metal programming, custom PCB design, and FPGA acceleration, my goal is to build nodes that don't just survive off-grid, but actively process and infer data where no cloud connection exists.
