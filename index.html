The Reconfigurable Edge Sentinel (RES) Platform

A modular family of ultra-low-power, infrastructure-independent embedded systems built to deploy advanced hardware capabilities where networks and grid power do not exist.

01

Co-Processing Matrix

Combines the architectural efficiency of an STM32H7 microcontroller with a Lattice iCE40 FPGA. Offload intensive filtering, acoustic analysis, and DSP routines directly to silicon.

02

Off-Grid LoRa Topology

Long-range telemetry engineered around Semtech SX1262 transceivers. Optimized layouts, custom helical matching networks, and robust codebases ensure packet tracking across extreme distances.

03

Aggressive Power Controls

Designed explicitly for smallholder configurations. Solar energy harvesting utilizing high-efficiency MPPT matching paired with rigorous hardware line isolation to drop deep sleep currents below 50 µA.

Shared HDL building blocks and firmware core deployed across highly targeted sensor variants.
